Welcome to ARIP

Ashok & Rita Patel Institute of Physiotherapy started its first academic session in the year of 2009-10. The evaluation system is based on Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) system and total credits to earn a Bachelor of Physiotherapy degree has 240 credits and Master of Physiotherapy degree has 100 credits.
